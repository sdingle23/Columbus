Destination Columbus!

Looking for a vacation destination or a great city to visit? Well Columbus, Ohio may be the place for you. I recently visited this great city a year ago for a graduation party, and instantly fell in love with it. Columbus is city full of life, energy, and opportunity waiting for adventurers to discover. Well known for its prestigious university Ohio State, the world renown Columbus Zoo, and for art lovers Cosi Museum, this city has something for everyone to enjoy. To give you an idea of how far Columbus has come since its inception, lets discuss a brief history of how it came to past.

History

Columbus was first designed in 1812 by of the Ohio State government, as the new state capital. That year groundbreaking efforts began to construct and start to build the city. It quickly began to grow with city lots being established and sold, to building of a penitentiary, school, church and a newspaper. Progress of Columbus was quickly moving ahead. In 1816 the capital was moved to Columbus, making it the new state capital. Based on its convenient location to major transportation routes, Columbus was becoming an economical and highly populated city. Towards the end of the 19th century, the city gained recognition as a learning mecca. Ohio State University and Capital University was had been founded, providing students with an excellent source of higher learning that exist to this day. From the late Nineteenth century until today the growth of Columbus has not stopped, making it the largest populated city in Ohio. Industrial growth, technology, cultural life, and its educational institutions make Columbus a booming city of promise and opportunity. Cultural offerings as the Columbus Museum of Art, Center for Ohio Science and Industry, and the Ohio Historical Society makes it a socially and cultural giant.

Take a Visit

So, I invite you to navigate through this site to gain a better prospective of what Columbus has to offer. It is a city with offerings of great food, sites, recreation and culture that can offer an experience of excitement, no matter what your interest is. I am much as I have, you will want to visit this emerging city or return if you have visited in the past.
